Learn How to Make Ice Cream

Don't fret; keep away the sweat with this cool recipe!

Strawberry

Ice Cream

Recipe

  • Prep Time:10min
  • Total Time: 6-8 hours
  • Difficulty:easy
  • Recipe Type:dessert
    Ingredients:
  • 14 ounces of condensed milk
  • 3/2 cups of heavy cream
  • 3/2 tablespoons of vanilla extract
  • 1/8 tablespoon of salt
  • 2 cups of strawberries

    Directions: Step 1 and 2

  • Chop some starberries to measure about 1/3 cup.
  • Place a bowl and a whisk inside a freezer to keep them cool.

    Directions: Step 3 and 4

  • Measure 3/2 cups of heavy cream and place it in the fridge.
  • Add 5/4 cups of strawberry into a food processor and puree them.

    Directions: Step 5 and 6

  • Add the salt, condensed milk, and vanilla extract to the pureed strawberries and mix well.
  • Remove the heavy cream and bowl form the fridge and freezer respectively and pour cream into chilled bowl. Mix cream until peaks form.

    Directions: Step 7 and 8

  • Scrape the whipped cream into the puree and fold them in on themselves until they combine. Do not mix.
  • Pour the mixture into a freezer-safe container and leave for 6-8 hours.

Enjoy!

Dig into that creamy, cool strawberry ice cream that you created from not but scratch!
